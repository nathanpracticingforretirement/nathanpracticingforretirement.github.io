Lessons learned from a Two Tour Bicycle Tourer

2021-08-25
It’s four am. I’m in the middle of Switzerland cowboy camping directly off a bicycle path in a small forested area. I’m laying on top of a tent, which we decided to use as a tarp as we were too tired and lazy to set it up, cursing the existence of god damn mosquitos, who buzzed around sounding like mini jet engines taking off. I’m wearing long johns under my pants, wool socks, shoes, a long sleeve, a puffer jacket, a beanie, a gator covering my face, three tshirts on top of my legs forming a shit makeshift blanket, a tshirt wrapped around my feet because those needle nose pricks are biting me through my wool socks.
